In the following task you have to decide as quickly as possible whether the font color of the words presented is green or blue.


Press A on your keyboard if the font color is green; press L on your keyboard if the font color is blue.

If a green check mark appears in the upper right corner, your decision was correct, otherwise a red cross will be displayed. Press the escape key (ESC) on your keyboard to exit the training at any time.

Start with the right arrow on your keyboard.
